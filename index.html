Our Mission

Honoring a Legacy,
Building a Future

The 6th Annual Kizzier Classic was created to honor the memory of Ryan Kizzier — a beloved family member, friend, and someone who brought people together.


This annual charity golf tournament brings together friends, family, and community to celebrate Ryan's spirit while raising funds for causes close to his heart. Every swing, every laugh, every moment on the course carries forward his legacy.
